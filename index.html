FREQUENTLY ASK QUESTION

To provide answers common questions about vaccination schedules, ensuring parents never miss essential immunizations for their child.

Q: Why is vaccination important for children?

Ans: Vaccination protects children from serious diseases and helps build immunity early in life.

Q: How will I know when my child needs a vaccine?

Ans: The vaccination alert system sends timely reminders based on your child's age and vaccination schedule.

Q: Are there side effects of vaccines?

Ans: Minor side effects like fever or soreness are common but serious reactions are rare. Always consult your doctor for advice.

Q: Where can I find a hospital for vaccination?

Ans: You can use the link to find nearby hospitals offering vaccination services.

Q: When should my child receive their first vaccine?

Ans: Vaccination usually starts at birth with the first dose of the hepatitis B vaccine.

Q: Are vaccinations safe for my child?

Ans: Yes, vaccines are rigorously tested for safety and are vital in preventing diseases.

Q: How can I track my child's vaccination schedule?

Ans: You can set up reminders through vaccination alert systems.
